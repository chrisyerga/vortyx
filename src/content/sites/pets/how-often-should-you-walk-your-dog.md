## The short answer

Most adult dogs do well with **two walks a day** totaling 30–60 minutes.
But the right number depends on age, breed group, and your individual dog's
energy level.

## By life stage

- **Puppies** — several short outings (5 minutes per month of age, up to twice a day).
- **Adults** — two walks daily; working and sporting breeds often need more.
- **Seniors** — keep the routine, shorten the distance, watch for stiffness.

## Signs you should adjust

If your dog is restless, destructive, or pacing in the evening, add time.
If they lag, sit down mid-walk, or limp afterward, scale back and talk to
your vet.
